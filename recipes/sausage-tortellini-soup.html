Sausage Tortellini Soup

Decription

Here's a recipe for sausage tortellini soup that I found online and love. It's super easy to make and tastes delicious. What more could you ask for?

Ingredients

The ingredients required are as follow:

  • 1 pound ground Italian sausage, hot or mild
  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon oregano
  • 1 pinch cayenne, optional
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 2 cups refrigerated tortellini
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Remove the casings from the sausage if you purchased links. Cook and crumble the sausage and diced onions over medium-high heat until the onions are softened and the sausage is cooked through, 8-10 minutes. Drain grease. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  2. Add the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes to remove the raw flour taste.
  3. Add basil, oregano, cayenne, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Stir to combine.
  4. Add the chicken broth and use a silicone spatula to “clean” the bottom of the pot. Bring to a boil, reduce to a simmer.
  5. Add the spinach and tortellini and simmer for 3-5 minutes. (If your tortellini needs longer cooking time, add the tortellini first, then the kale during the last 5 minutes.)
  6. Taste the soup and add salt if desired. Start with ¼ tsp and add more after that if needed.
  7. Transfer to serving bowls and serve!
